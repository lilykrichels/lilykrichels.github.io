About Me

Before deciding to enter the exciting world of software development, I worked primarily in leadership positions at hospitality and nonprofit organizations. Although I enjoyed the challenge of connecting with people and meeting business goals, continuing my education had always been in the back of my mind.

Software development appeals to me because it is creativity-driven functionality. Coding is a skill that can be used in nearly every industry, allowing for enormous opportunity and flexibility. I enjoy the challenge of making applications that not only work seamlessly, but also provide the best user experience possible.

I write clean, organized code, and am excited by the prospect of developing my career in Cleveland. I have a particular interest in front-end languages, but am eager to learn more about all aspects of software development.

Bank Account Application

In this project, I designed a console application to manage a bank account. I chose to make my client Scrooge McDuck and gave him a starting balance of $31.2 billion, the estimated value of the piles of gold he likes to swim in. The bank account has many of the same functionalities as an ATM and allows the client to access checking and savings account balances, as well as make withdrawals or deposits. In addition, the user has a minimum account balance and is charged a transaction fee whenever a withdrawal from savings is made.
